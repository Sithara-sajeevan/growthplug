Dr. Barret Davidson is thrilled to provide family and cosmetic dental services to patients in the communities located in and around McKinney, TX. As the owner of Texas Sage Dentistry, Dr. Davidson is equipped with advanced skills and expertise to perform general, cosmetic, and restorative dental procedures and treatments that meet your oral care needs.
